VidSum Extension

YouTube Video Analyzer

Segments, Summary, Settings are in the floating panel on a video page (youtube.com/watch?v=…). If you closed it with × on the panel, turn Show floating panel on again here.

This extension does not open the VidSum website. To sign in, open VidSum in a normal tab (paste the URL above into the address bar).
